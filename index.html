How the Inquiry Works:

  • Select a Mentor: Choose one of three alumni from the global network to begin a consultation.
  • Identify Skills: Distinguish the three core Humanistic Skills that underpin their specific professional role.

  • Complete the Cycle: You must successfully authenticate three distinct mentors to unlock the final career analysis.
  • The Reward: Once three inquiries are complete, choose one mentor to receive a personal three-paragraph letter detailing their career pathway from 2025 to 2045.
